# ASAR SERVICE DEFINITIONS

## Parking Lot Maintenance
**NOT general pressure washing of parking lots.**
Specific services:
- **Parking lot line striping** (fresh striping, re-striping, ADA compliance markings, directional arrows, fire lane markings)
- **Pavement sealcoating** (asphalt sealcoating to protect and extend pavement life)
- **Pavement repair** (crack filling, pothole patching, surface restoration)

Competitor set: pavement maintenance companies, striping contractors, asphalt companies — NOT pressure washing companies.
Keywords: "parking lot striping Conway AR", "asphalt sealcoating Little Rock", "parking lot repair Arkansas", "pavement marking Conway"

## Fleet Washing
Mobile fleet washing service — goes to the customer's yard/depot.
NOT a fixed truck wash location like Blue Beacon or Love's.
Target customer: fleet operators, trucking companies, delivery companies, municipal fleets, construction equipment fleets.
