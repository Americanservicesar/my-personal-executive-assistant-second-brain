Job photos and videos go in **MARKETING shared drive > Job Photos & Videos** (folder ID: `1AgXMJgHSN4T2ULexdLB2RrqePMWPFTtc`), organized by service subfolder.
URL: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1AgXMJgHSN4T2ULexdLB2RrqePMWPFTtc

**Folder structure**: Service type → sub-category (e.g., Pressure Washing > Parking Lots)
Services: Pressure Washing, Fleet Washing, Gutter Services, Construction Cleanup, Parking Lot Maintenance, Window Cleaning, Soft Washing, Holiday Lighting

**File naming**: `YYYY-MM-DD_[Service]_[City]_[PropertyType]_[BeforeOrAfter]_[Number].ext`
Example: `2026-04-04_PressureWash_Conway_ApartmentComplex_After_01.jpg`

**Why:** Soshie needs searchable, organized media to pull for social posts. Labeling by service + city enables GBP city rotation and segment-specific content.

**How to apply:** When saving any job media to Drive, always save to the correct service subfolder in MARKETING > Job Photos & Videos with the naming convention above. Never dump photos in the root or personal Drive.
