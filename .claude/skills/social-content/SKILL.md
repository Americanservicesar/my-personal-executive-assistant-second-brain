## Workflow

### Step 1 — Clarify the Content Need

Options:
- **Single post:** One-off post for a specific platform
- **Content calendar:** Weekly or monthly plan across platforms
- **Campaign:** Coordinated multi-post series (seasonal, promotional)
- **Engagement response:** Reply to comments, reviews, messages

### Step 2 — Check Memory

Before creating:
1. Check `memory/marketing.md` → Social Media Insights for what performs best
2. Check `memory/marketing.md` → Content That Works / Flopped
3. Check seasonal patterns: what services are hot right now?

### Step 3 — Create Content by Type

**Before/After Posts (highest engagement):**
- Photo: side-by-side or swipe
- Caption: Brief description of the job + result
- Hashtags: #PressureWashing #ConwayAR #CommercialCleaning
- CTA: "DM us for a free estimate" or "Link in bio"

**Job Highlights:**
- Photo of crew or equipment on-site
- Caption: "Another [service] completed at [type of property] in [city]"
- Tag location

**Educational Tips:**
- "3 signs your gutters need replacing"
- "Why your parking lot needs pressure washing before summer"
- Position ASAR as the expert

**Testimonials / Reviews:**
- Screenshot or designed graphic of 5-star review
- Caption thanking the client
- CTA: "Want results like these?"

**Seasonal Promotions:**
- Coordinate with Commet for package pricing
- Coordinate with Emmie for email cross-promotion
- Time-limited offers with clear CTA

### Step 4 — Content Calendar Template

| Week | Monday | Wednesday | Friday |
|------|--------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | Before/After (FB+IG) | Tip/Education (all) | Job Highlight (FB+IG) |
| 2 | Testimonial (all) | Seasonal Promo (all) | Behind-the-scenes (IG) |
| 3 | Before/After (FB+IG) | GBP Update | Job Highlight (FB+IG) |
| 4 | Review Highlight (all) | Blog share (FB+LI) | Monthly recap (all) |

### Step 5 — Post & Track

1. Schedule via Service Robot (GHL) social scheduler where possible
2. For platforms without GHL integration: deliver ready-to-post content with formatting
3. After posting: track engagement in `memory/marketing.md`

## Learning Protocol

1. **Before creating:** Check `memory/marketing.md` → Social Media Insights for best post types per platform
2. **After posting:** Log post type, platform, and initial engagement to `memory/marketing.md`
3. **After 48 hours:** Update with final engagement numbers (likes, comments, shares, clicks)
4. **Track leads from social:** If a post generates an inquiry, log source to `memory/clients.md` → Lead Conversion Funnel
5. **Monthly:** Review which post types and platforms drove the most leads (not just likes)
6. **If post flops:** Log to Content That Flopped with analysis of why
