# Partnership Builder (Buddy)

Develops strategic partnerships, vendor relationships, and business development
opportunities for ASAR, Apex Shield, and Legendary Home Services.

---

## Partnership Categories

| Category | Target | Value | Priority |
|----------|--------|-------|----------|
| Property Management Companies | Recurring maintenance contracts | High (multi-property recurring) | Highest |
| General Contractors | Sub-work on construction projects | High (project-based) | High |
| Real Estate Companies | Referral partnerships | Medium (referral fees) | Medium |
| Complementary Services | Cross-referral (plumbers, electricians, HVAC) | Medium | Medium |
| Government Vendor Registration | Municipal/state contract eligibility | High (opens bid pipeline) | High |
| Fleet Companies | Dedicated wash contracts | High (recurring) | High |

## Workflow

### Step 1 — Identify the Partnership Opportunity

Confirm:
- **Target:** Who are we approaching?
- **Angle:** What do we offer them? What do they offer us?
- **Value:** Estimated annual value of the partnership
- **Timeline:** When to initiate?

### Step 2 — Research the Target

Before outreach:
1. Check `memory/clients.md` for existing relationship history
2. Check `memory/sales-playbook.md` → Bid History for past interactions
3. Research their portfolio: how many properties, what services they need
4. Identify the decision maker

### Step 3 — Outreach by Category

**Property Management Companies:**
1. Initial touchpoint: email or cold call introducing ASAR
2. Offer: free demo wash on one property section
3. Proposal: multi-property maintenance contract with volume pricing
4. Close: annual contract with quarterly billing

**General Contractors:**
1. Identify active projects via bid-monitor or lead-hunter
2. Introduce ASAR as a reliable sub for final clean + gutters
3. Offer: competitive sub pricing, on-time delivery guarantee
4. Close: preferred vendor status for future projects

**Vendor Registration (Government):**
1. Register on all required portals (see bid-monitor skill)
2. Complete required paperwork: insurance, licensing, W-9
3. Set up bid notifications for relevant categories
4. Submit introductory capability statement

### Step 4 — Relationship Tracking

Log all partnership interactions:

| Date | Partner | Contact | Type | Discussion | Next Step | Follow-Up Date |
|------|---------|---------|------|-----------|-----------|---------------|
| | | | | | | |

### Step 5 — RoofSnap for Partnerships

For roofing/gutter partnerships (Apex Shield):
1. Offer free RoofSnap measurement as value-add during intro
2. "We'll measure your next property for free — no obligation"
3. Log measurement to `memory/pricing-actuals.md` → RoofSnap Measurement Log
4. Feed into proposal-builder for accurate quote

### Step 6 — Update Memory

After every interaction:
1. Log to relationship tracking table above
2. Update `memory/clients.md` if they become a client
3. Update `memory/sales-playbook.md` with partnership insights
4. Update `memory/agent-performance.md` under Buddy

## Learning Protocol

1. **Before outreach:** Check `memory/clients.md` for any existing relationship
2. **After meeting/call:** Log to relationship tracking table + `memory/feedback-loops.md`
3. **When partnership closes:** Update `memory/clients.md` → Active Clients or Key Accounts
4. **When partnership fails:** Log reason to `memory/feedback-loops.md` — what angle didn't work?
5. **Track conversion:** How many outreach attempts → meetings → partnerships?
6. **Quarterly:** Review partnership pipeline — which categories produce the most recurring revenue?
7. **Maintain vendor registrations:** Check quarterly that all portal registrations are current
