## Common Objections & Best Responses

| Objection | Best Response | Notes |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| "Too expensive" | Focus on ROI — cost of NOT maintaining vs. our price. One-time deep clean + maintenance contract saves them money long-term. | |
| "We already have someone" | "How happy are you with them on a scale of 1-10?" — anything below 8, there's an opening. Offer a free demo wash on one section. | |
| "Not in the budget right now" | Offer seasonal scheduling — book now for next quarter at today's rate. Or break into smaller phases. | |
| "Send me some info" | "I'd rather show you — can I swing by for 5 minutes? I'll show you exactly what we'd do and what it costs. No pressure." | |
| "We handle it in-house" | "What's your fully-loaded cost per hour for that crew? Most facilities save 30-40% outsourcing to us." | |
