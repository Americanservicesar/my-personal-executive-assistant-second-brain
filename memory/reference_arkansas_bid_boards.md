## 10. QUICK-START CHECKLIST FOR BID MONITORING

1. **Register on SAM.gov** (free, required for federal work, takes ~2 weeks)
2. **Register on AR DFA Vendor Self-Service** (state contracts)
3. **Register on BidNet Direct** (free, catches many AR local government bids)
4. **Register on Bonfire/ProcureNow** (free, used by several AR entities)
5. **Bookmark DFA Bids & Solicitations page** — check weekly
6. **Bookmark your top 10 city/county procurement pages** — check weekly
7. **Sign up for DPSAFT notifications** (school construction)
8. **Set up SAM.gov saved searches** for AR + your NAICS codes
9. **Subscribe to AR Democrat-Gazette legal notices** (catches newspaper-only postings)
10. **Contact county judges directly** for the 50+ counties without online portals — ask to be on their bid notification mailing list
