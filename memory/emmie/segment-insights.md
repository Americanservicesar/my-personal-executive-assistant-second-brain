## ASAR-01-Apartments -- Priority: HIGHEST
- Services: Pressure washing (parking + dumpster pads), gutter cleaning, window cleaning
- Decision maker: Property manager / maintenance director
- Best pain point: Tenant complaints about appearance, curb appeal for lease renewals
- Best CTA: "Can I show you what clean looks like at [property name]?"
- Lead with: Appearance = tenant retention = fewer vacancies
- Campaign: Draft -- leads export needed before launch

## ASAR-02-HOA -- Priority: HIGH
- Services: Pressure washing (common areas, driveways), gutter cleaning, window cleaning
- Decision maker: HOA board president / property management company
- Best pain point: Neighbor complaints, curb appeal enforcement, board liability
- Best CTA: "We service HOAs across Central AR -- can I schedule a walk-through?"
- Lead with: HOA outsources because in-house = inconsistency + board conflict
- Campaign: Not started

## ASAR-03-CommercialPropMgmt -- Priority: HIGHEST
- Services: Pressure washing, gutter cleaning, window cleaning, parking lot, permanent lighting
- Decision maker: Commercial property manager / facility director
- Best pain point: Multiple vendors = coordination headache. One call for everything.
- Best CTA: "What properties are you managing in Central AR?"
- Lead with: One call handles everything -- exterior maintenance + permanent lighting
- Note: One of only 2 segments with permanent lighting
- Campaign: Not started

## ASAR-04-Fleet -- Priority: HIGH
- Services: Fleet washing -- mobile, we come to their yard (NOT a fixed truck wash)
- Decision maker: Fleet manager / operations director
- Best pain point: Driver downtime at fixed washes, DOT appearance standards
- Best CTA: "We come to your yard -- zero fleet downtime"
- Lead with: Mobile premium -- we come to them, no driver trips
- HCP actual: 127 jobs, $763/visit, 78% margin -- CORE revenue
- Campaign: Not started

## ASAR-05-Warehouse -- Priority: HIGH
- Services: Pressure washing (dock doors, concrete, exterior), fleet washing, parking lot
- Decision maker: Facility manager / plant manager
- Best pain point: OSHA compliance on dock pads, slip hazards, quarterly maintenance windows
- Best CTA: "Can we schedule a walk-through for your next maintenance window?"
- Lead with: Compliance + safety, not just appearance
- Campaign: Not started

## ASAR-06-GeneralContractors -- Priority: MEDIUM
- Services: Construction cleanup (final clean + COC), pressure washing, window cleaning
- Decision maker: Project manager / superintendent
- Best pain point: Needs COC-ready cleaner who shows up on time, every time
- Best CTA: "What projects are in final clean phase right now?"
- Lead with: Reliability + COC experience -- no-show cleaner kills punch-list
- Campaign: Not started

## ASAR-07-Government -- Priority: HIGH
- Services: Pressure washing, parking lot, fleet washing, building exterior
- Decision maker: Facilities director / procurement officer
- Best pain point: Annual bid requirements, budget cycles, certified vendor lists
- Best CTA: "Are you on AR Bid Online? We are a registered vendor."
- Lead with: Procurement compliance -- we know the bid process
- Buddy monitors: 86 AR bid portals including SAM.gov
- Campaign: Not started

## ASAR-08-Schools -- Priority: MEDIUM
- Services: Pressure washing, gutter cleaning, parking lot, window cleaning
- Decision maker: Facilities director / superintendent
- Best pain point: Summer maintenance window is short -- no-show = disaster
- Best CTA: "When does your summer maintenance window open?"
- Lead with: Summer scheduling + on-time reliability
- Campaign: Not started

## ASAR-09-UniHospital -- Priority: MEDIUM
- Services: Pressure washing, window cleaning, parking lot, fleet washing
- Decision maker: Facilities manager / VP Operations
- Best pain point: Appearance standards for accreditation, patient/visitor experience
- Best CTA: "We work with medical and educational facilities -- can I send a scope?"

- Campaign: Not started

## ASAR-10-Dealerships -- Priority: MEDIUM
- Services: Pressure washing (lot + showroom), fleet washing, permanent lighting, window cleaning
- Decision maker: General manager / facilities manager
- Best pain point: Dirty lot Saturday morning = lost floor traffic
- Best CTA: "What does your lot look like when customers pull in Saturday morning?"
- Lead with: Lot appearance drives floor traffic drives sales
- Note: One of only 2 segments with permanent lighting
- Campaign: Not started

## ASAR-11-Hotels -- Priority: MEDIUM
- Services: Pressure washing (entrance, parking, pool deck), window cleaning, gutter cleaning
- Decision maker: General manager / maintenance director
- Best pain point: OTA reviews mention cleanliness -- curb appeal affects booking scores
- Best CTA: "What does your TripAdvisor page say about exterior cleanliness?"
- Lead with: Review score = occupancy rate = revenue per room
- Campaign: Not started

## ASAR-12-Restaurants -- Priority: LOW

- Decision maker: Owner / regional manager
- Best pain point: Health inspection compliance, grease buildup
- Best CTA: "When is your next health inspection?"
- Lead with: Health code compliance, not just appearance
