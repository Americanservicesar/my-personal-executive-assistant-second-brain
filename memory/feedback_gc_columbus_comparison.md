## Lesson
Columbus workbook has two notable anomalies:
1. Motor grader severely underpriced at $575 (should be $700-1,000) — likely owner-operated old equipment, not market rate
2. Water trailer at $450 is ~50-100% above RS Means — likely inflated to cover fuel/operator. Do not use Columbus water rates.
Always check motor grader and water equipment rates against RS Means when reviewing outside workbooks.
