ASAR's presence on lead generation platforms (Thumbtack, Angi, HomeAdvisor, Yelp, Nextdoor, Houzz, BBB, etc.) is for SEO citation building and backlinks ONLY.

**Why:** NAP consistency across directories builds domain authority and local SEO signals. These platforms are NOT a paid lead channel for ASAR.

**How to apply:**
- When setting up or optimizing any of these profiles, never enable paid lead packages, ad spend, or budget settings
- Keep profiles complete (NAP, services, photos, hours) for citation value
- Do not recommend or configure any paid placement on these platforms
- Thumbtack specifically: FREE tier only — profile exists for backlink/citation value
