RoofSnap and GutterGlove measurements are NOT used for gutter cleaning jobs.

**Why:** Gutter cleaning pricing is based on sqft tiers (auto-calculated by the Address Processor / Estimate Engine). Linear footage is only needed for installation, repair, and guards where material cost depends on total footage.

**How to apply:**
- Gutter cleaning: sqft-based tier pricing only. No RoofSnap order, no GutterGlove link.
- Gutter installation / repair / guards: GutterGlove pre-measure link required before quoting. RoofSnap order may be used for precision measurement.
