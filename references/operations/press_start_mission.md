## Anthony's Real-World Prompt Library

These are the EXACT things Anthony must do personally — agents cannot do these for him.

### Sales (Milli hands these off)
**When Milli flags a hot lead for site visit:**
> "I have a site visit at [Property] on [Date/Time]. I'm meeting [Name], [Title]. They're interested in [Service]. My goal is to close on a maintenance contract. Milli — prep my site visit checklist and talking points."

**When a proposal is ready to sign:**
> Anthony signs the service agreement in person or DocuSign — agents cannot execute contracts.

**When a deal is over $5,000:**
> Dexter flags it → Anthony approves the margin before Milli quotes.

### Finance
**Weekly (Monday morning):**
> "Dexter — run my weekly KPI report. Include: pipeline value, jobs completed, revenue vs last week, top 3 opportunities, any margin alerts."

**When Dexter flags a cash flow issue:**
> Review QuickBooks directly — agents can read but not move money.

### Operations
**When Vizzy schedules a won job:**
> Anthony assigns the crew in Housecall Pro — agents cannot dispatch crews.

**When a crew needs supplies:**
> Anthony approves purchase orders — agents can generate the PO but Anthony must approve.

### Marketing Budget
**Monthly:**
> "Emmie — how much budget do we need for next month's email campaigns? Soshie — what's the social ad budget recommendation?"
> Anthony approves and loads budget into Google Ads / Facebook Ads manually.

### Reviews & Reputation
**After every completed job:**
> Review request goes automatically (n8n workflow already live).
> If a negative review comes in → Cassie drafts the response → **Anthony posts it** (agents cannot post publicly without approval).

### Legal / Compliance
> Any contract signing, tax filing, license renewal — Anthony only.
