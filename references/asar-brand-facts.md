# American Services AR — Brand Facts

## Founded
Serving Central Arkansas since **2015**

## Service Area
Central Arkansas (Little Rock metro, Conway, Bryant, Benton, Sherwood, North Little Rock, Cabot, Maumelle, and surrounding areas)

## Brands
- American Services AR (ASAR) — primary residential + commercial
- Apex Shield Coatings — coatings division
- Legendary Exterior Solutions — exterior division

```css

  --navy:   #0050A0;  /* primary blue */
```

**How identified:** PIL pixel analysis of logo file (Drive ID: `1Erx00BjvdSjFAXIceoZ-MYpeeYxCyHqL`). Blue = 55% of non-white pixels. Red = 12%. These are BRIGHTER than official US flag Pantone values — use these, not `#002868`/`#BF0A30`.
